What's a Shiny Pokemon?

A Shiny Pokémon is a rare and uniquely colored variant of a regular Pokémon, distinguished by its distinct color palette and sparkling entry animation. Unlike their standard counterparts, Shiny Pokémon have a different hue, making them highly sought after by trainers for their rarity. The odds of encountering a Shiny Pokémon are extremely low, adding an element of excitement and prestige to capturing them.
